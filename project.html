Project Overview

The Shareholder Management System (SMS) is designed to automate and streamline the management of shareholder records, share transactions, corporate actions, and compliance reporting.

Key Features

  • Shareholder Registry
  • Share Management
  • Corporate Actions
  • Reporting
  • Document Management
  • Access Control
  • Communication

Project Progress

We are continuously working on improving the system. Here are some of the upcoming features:

  • Enhanced Reporting Capabilities
  • Advanced Analytics
  • Mobile App Integration
